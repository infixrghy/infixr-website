Solo VR training scales reach. Live, facilitated cohorts scale *judgment* — the
kind that only shows up when people react to each other under pressure.

## The setup

Twelve trainees, one shared virtual space, a facilitator who could pause the
scenario and replay any moment from any angle. The replay is the lever: "watch
what you did" beats "here is what you should have done."

## What surprised us

The quiet trainees spoke up more in VR than in a classroom. Presence without the
full social weight of a room lowered the cost of trying.
