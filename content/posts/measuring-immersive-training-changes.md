A training program you cannot measure is a program you cannot defend at renewal
time. Immersive training has a quiet advantage here: the headset already knows
what the learner did.

## The metrics that matter

We instrument three layers:

1. **Completion** — did they finish, and where did they drop off?
2. **Performance** — did they hit the procedure correctly, and how fast?
3. **Transfer** — did on-the-job incident rates move after rollout?

The third is the one that renews contracts. Everything else is leading indicators
for it.
