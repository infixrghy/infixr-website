A pilot proves the idea works. A rollout proves your *operation* can run it at
scale. The gap between the two is where most VR programs quietly stall.

## What breaks at scale

- **Hardware logistics** — charging, hygiene, and tracking dozens of headsets.
- **Content updates** — a fix has to reach every device without a re-deploy circus.
- **Support** — someone has to own the program after the launch excitement fades.

We design the rollout plan alongside the pilot, not after it, so scaling is a
gear change rather than a restart.
