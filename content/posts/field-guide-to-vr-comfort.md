Motion sickness in VR is not inevitable — it is a design failure with well-known
fixes. We treat comfort as a ship-blocker, not a nice-to-have.

## The comfort checklist

- **Never move the camera the user did not ask to move.** Forced acceleration is
  the fastest route to nausea.
- **Keep a stable horizon.** A fixed reference frame settles the inner ear.
- **Hold frame rate above the headset's floor**, every frame, no exceptions.

Run the checklist on every build. Comfort earns the trust that lets people train
for longer.
